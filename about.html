About

What this is

The Filings Digest publishes short, source-linked notes on new SEC filings from a fixed watchlist of shipping, steel and mining companies. An AI model drafts each note. A mechanical checker verifies every quote before publication. The methodology page explains both steps.

Access

Paid access is launching soon. Until then, new issues appear as free previews with headlines only, and open in full after 7 days.
