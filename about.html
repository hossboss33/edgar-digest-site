How it works

A watcher checks SEC EDGAR each weekday for new filings from a fixed watchlist of shipping, steel and mining companies. An AI model (Claude) drafts a short note for each filing under a strict rule: every factual bullet must carry an exact, verbatim quote from the filing text.

A separate, non-AI verification step then checks each quote character-for-character against the filing, and checks that every number in a bullet appears in its supporting quote. Only notes that pass appear here. Anything flagged is quarantined and never published.
