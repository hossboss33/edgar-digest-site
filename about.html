About

What this is

The Filings Digest publishes short, source-linked notes on new SEC filings from a watchlist of the 500 largest U.S. filers plus a seeded shipping and steel roster. An AI model drafts each note. A mechanical checker verifies every quote before publication. The methodology page explains both steps.

Access

Subscriptions ($5 a month) are launching soon. Until then, new issues appear as free previews with headlines only, and open in full after 7 days.

You can follow specific companies two ways: star them on the companies page (your browser keeps the list, and nothing is sent anywhere), or subscribe to the RSS feed for every new issue.
